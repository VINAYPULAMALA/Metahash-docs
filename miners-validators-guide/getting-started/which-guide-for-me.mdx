## TL;DR Decision Matrix

| Role | Start with… | Core outcomes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Miners bringing a first rig online or automating multiple bids | [Miner Guide](/miners-validators-guide/miners/miner-advanced-guide) | Copy-paste bootstrap, spend controls, payment routing, automation hooks |
| Validators preparing to accept auctions or hardening existing infrastructure | [Validator Guide](/miners-validators-guide/validators/validator-advanced-guide) | Environment prep, process managers, strategy tuning, telemetry, redundancy |

## Guided Flow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Identify Your Role">
    <ul>
      <li><strong>Miner</strong> — Converting α to META through auctions.</li>
      <li><strong>Validator</strong> — Clearing auctions, publishing commitments, managing treasuries.</li>
      <li><strong>Explorer</strong> — Still learning? Start with the <a href="/welcome/overview">Welcome overview</a>.</li>
    </ul>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Match Experience Level">
    <p>The playbooks open with copy-paste quickstarts and branch into automation, redundancy, and monitoring. Skim the headings and dive deepest where your operations need it.</p>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Readiness">
    <ul>
      <li>Run <code>btcli wallet list</code> to confirm your hotkey exists.</li>
      <li>Check validator permits with <code>btcli subnet list --netuid 73</code> if you plan to accept bids.</li>
      <li>Ensure hardware meets the <a href="/miners-validators-guide/getting-started/introduction">environment prerequisites</a> and required ports are open.</li>
    </ul>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Final Checklist

- [ ] Hardware, OS, and networking match the specs in the environment guide.
- [ ] Wallets and treasuries are backed up, and you know how to validate them with `treasuries.py`.
- [ ] You have Discord access for real-time support if something looks off.

Still unsure? Share your plan in the MetaHash Discord. The community will flag gaps and point you to the safest starting point before you ship.
