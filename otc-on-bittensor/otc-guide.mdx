</div>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Staging" icon="rocket">
    **Validators set the stage**: Publish META budgets, α capacity caps, and discount rate limits to establish auction parameters for the epoch.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Bidding" icon="coins">
    **Miners submit offers**: α holders bid their tokens with desired discount rates (in basis points), competing for the best liquidity terms.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clearing" icon="check-circle">
    **Validators execute matching**: Rank all submitted bids by discount rate, allocate available META to winning bids, and finalize trade pairs deterministically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Settlement" icon="repeat">
    **On-chain exchange**: α tokens and META liquidity are swapped atomically through the Treasury smart contract with full transparency.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Redistribution" icon="recycle">
    **Treasury recycling**: Collected yield flows back into the ecosystem through META buybacks, validator rewards, and miner incentives — completing the loop.
  </Step>
</Steps>
