## How It Works

<div style={{
  padding: '1.5rem',
  background: 'rgba(255, 140, 0, 0.08)',
  border: '1px solid rgba(255, 140, 0, 0.2)',
  borderRadius: '8px',
  marginBottom: '1.5rem'
}}>
  MetaHash's OTC layer runs through <strong>deterministic Epoch auctions</strong>, ensuring predictable liquidity and verifiable settlement.
</div>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Staging">
    Validators publish META budgets, α caps, and discount limits.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Bidding">
    Miners submit α + discount bids (bps).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clearing">
    Validators rank bids, allocate META, and finalize trades.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Settlement">
    α and META exchange on-chain through the Treasury.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Redistribution">
    The Treasury recycles yield into buybacks and rewards.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Treasury Ecosystem

<Tip>
The Treasury acts as MetaHash's coordination hub — connecting miners, validators, and funds through OTC deals, buybacks, and redistribution.
</Tip>

## MetaHash Liquidity Loop

<Note>
**α flows from miners to the Treasury, META flows back as liquidity.**

Validators clear auctions, funds deploy capital, and the Treasury recycles yield through buybacks and rewards — creating a self-balancing OTC economy.
</Note>
